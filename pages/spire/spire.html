Fight Building a character Rolling

Core mechanics

Rolling Dice

Rolls are made with D10, add or remove dice to a poll depending on the circumstances.

1: Critical Failure
2-5: Failure (take stress)
6-7: Succes at a cost (take stress)
8-9: Success (take no stress)
10: Critical succes (inflict +1 stress for each 10 you roll)

Adding/removing dice to pool

When a roll is called for there are three things to consider regarding the number of dice to roll

  • The characters skill level/proficiency
  • The difficulty
  • Solo versus group
Character skill level
Have the needed skill: Add D10
Have the needed domain: Add D10
Have mastery (skill/action/domain) Add D10 (This does NOT stack)
Difficulty level
Standard Remove 1D10
Challening Remove 1D10 (to a minimum of 1D10)
Very challening Remove 2D10 (to a minimum of 1D10)
Group/assisted rolls
Assisted Add 1D10 to dice pool for each player with skill/domain that assists (limit set by GM) Note: assisting players takes stress as well
Group One player takes lead, and rolls regularly On success everyone else rolls with mastery
